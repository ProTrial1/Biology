1. Plant Taxonomy

Over 80% of all known living plant species are Angiosperms. Mastering the identification of flowers and fruits is your highest-leverage skill.

  • ■Angiosperms: Flowering plants. Seeds enclosed in an ovary.
  • ■Gymnosperms: Conifers, cycads. "Naked seeds".
  • ■Pteridophytes: Ferns. Reproduce via spores.
